Networking

ICS offers broadband wireless communications products and technologies, offering businesses including telecom service providers, governments and enterprises effective, economic and secure high-speed wireless communications solutions. ICS specializes in high-speed Internet access, VoIP, data network extension, and wireless data and telephony backhaul, utilizing its high quality products and industry-leading technologies. We also provide Point-to-Point and Point-to-Multipoint connectivity for various corporates, Government semi and Quasi Government Organizations, including defence sector. We provide complete Wifi Solutions for Business groups like Mining, Defence, Oil Refineries, traffic surveillance and various other security based applications including video and data. We undertake the projects on turkey basis, including site survey, suggest solution, conduct demonstration of the product / solution, installation and commissioning. ICS a further supports its clients by supplying the peripheral items like cables (cat 5, cat 5 e, armoured, shielded) as per the site requirement and also the masts of 15 M, 21 M as per site conditions with all connected details including the mast foundation. ICS is pleased to enter in to a memorandum of understanding with M/s. Vertel Communications Systems Pvt. Ltd., New Delhi for the supply of Licence exempt broadband products such as Breeze Acees, Breeze Net, Breeze Max and etc. M/s. Vertel Communications is the Indian Distributors for the Broadband Products of Alvarion, Israel. Now ICS is a System Integrator and provides solutions for video security and surveillance systems, data communications and last mile connectivity using the Alvarion Broadband Products.
