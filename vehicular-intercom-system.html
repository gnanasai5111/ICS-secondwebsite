The System Intercom unit provides the selection and switching between communication links VHF, HF and Line, SMART and two operators. This interfacing unit also provides the audio Recording & Replay using peripheral Recorder. Maximum of two operators can operate the system. The operators have option to interface with VHF, HF, Line and SMART one at a time based on the selection from the LCD selection keys.

Vehicular Intercom System Features

Vehicular Intercom System

  • Full duplex audio Intercom communication for two operators and External communication system.
  • Supporting maximum of two Radios.
  • Accessibility for the commander to communicate with Operators.
  • Provision for base band modulated data communication.
  • Recording facility for conference call.
  • The system supports voice and data communication between 2 crew members, 1 Commander and 2 external radios.
  • Data communication through peripheral SMART device.
  • Facility record and Replay using peripheral recorder.
